Austin Dance Festival May 29, 2021 at Pioneer Farms


Austin Dance Festival 2021 is going outdoors! Discover local modern dance companies as they perform live throughout the grounds of Pioneer Farms. Join our community group dance on the town square. Watch as our Youth Edition dancers take a master class in site-specific improvisation and then create a spontaneous performance. Settle in for Dance on Film which will be shown on Rocket Cinema’s 40-foot screen in an open field, under the Texas stars. All guests should bring their own blankets or chairs and a picnic (and maybe some insect repellent!). Covid rules will be enforced.

FAQ

WHAT IS PIONEER FARMS AND WHERE DO I PARK?

Pioneer Farms is a living history museum located in Northeast Austin, just ten minutes from Central Austin. We are hosting the festival at Pioneer Farms this year due to the pandemic. It is a scenic outdoor events venue that allows us to safely present dance in numerous sites throughout the property. There are two large, free parking lots on site.

WHAT SAFETY PROTOCOLS WILL BE IN PLACE?

This is an outdoor event in a spacious setting with plenty of room for people to practice social-distancing. We will require advanced ticket sales, restrict the number of tickets sold, and provide contactless check-in from your car. We will also enforce safety protocols as determined by the venue and by the City of Austin, such as masking up when social-distancing is not possible. These protocols can change often, so please check back for updates.

WHAT KIND OF DANCES AM I GOING TO SEE?

We usually host dance companies from all over the country in a theater designed specifically for viewing dance, but this year’s festival will feature Austin’s local professional and pre-professional dance companies performing contemporary modern dances on porches, in yards, and on 8x8 platforms. Since each dance reflects what is important to that particular choreographer, our programming is incredibly diverse. Look for expression, meaning, abstraction, virtuosity, minimalism, humor, gravity, grace, design, experimentation, athleticism, movement invention, and even more out-of-the-box thinking than usual!

CAN I BRING MY FAMILY?

Yes, absolutely! This is a family-friendly event! Most of the dances run 10 minutes or less. We suggest a PG-13 rating for our Dance on Film screening, but all of the dance performances leading up to the screening are suitable for all ages.

WHAT ELSE SHOULD I BRING?

Bring a picnic with plenty of water, drinks and snacks, a blanket and/or chairs, your mask, and insect repellent (just in case). Don’t bring alcohol or pets. Smoking is not permitted.

WHAT ELSE CAN I DO AT THE FESTIVAL?

In addition to watching performances by professional and pre-professional dance companies, you can watch a pop-up dance on the town square, take part in an improvisation class, watch dance on film on a big outdoor screen, buy a t-shirt, and have a picnic!
